The Story

I didn't find this path.
I was broken into it.

There was a version of me that smiled through everything while something inside was quietly falling apart. I looked fine from the outside. I had no idea what was happening underneath.

The truth is — I was disconnected from myself. From love. From the thing that makes life feel like it means something. And I kept performing life instead of actually living it.

"When I finally stopped running from my own pain — everything changed."

That's what this work is about. Not fixing you. Not giving you a 5-step plan. Walking with you into the part of yourself you've been avoiding — and showing you what's on the other side.

I've sat with hundreds of people in their worst moments. And I can tell you — the ones who go in, come out different. Alive. Purposeful. Themselves.
